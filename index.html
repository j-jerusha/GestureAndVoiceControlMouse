About The Project

GestureFlow is a Python-based application that leverages the power of computer vision and machine learning to translate your hand gestures into real-time cursor movements. Forget your mouse; control your computer with a wave of your hand. Integrated with voice commands, it offers a truly seamless and futuristic way to interact with your digital world.
